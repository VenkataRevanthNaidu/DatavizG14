Baltimore City Crime Visulization

Geo Map: Using leaflet and coordinates of Baltimore city, we have created the street level map of Baltimore city. The scatter plot is designed for interaction. Coming to the interaction, we have kept a mouse over in a scatter plot where a user can see the probability and intensity, and then the selected location will appear on the map. The user can also filter the data by choosing the locations in the map based on their required areas, and then the places will be filtered in the scatter plot.Location name, and the crime which occured most will be displayed on the map if we click a particular hotspot.

Line chart:We have selected the significant hotspots which have more trends. We are trying to show overall trends of all six years. Initially, we planned to create an interaction for the line chart, and we are currently working on that; once we complete that, we will update the website. The interaction we thought to do is while we click on a hotspot in the scatter plot, both map and the line chart should be changed. The map should display the location with impact and intensity, and the line chart should show the overall trend of the location from six years.
